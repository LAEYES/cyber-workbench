# STD — D12 — Business Continuity Plan (BCP) Standard

**Organization:** ACME  
**Version:** 0.2 (hardened)  
**Date:** 2026-02-03

## 1. Baseline requirements
- BIA: critical processes, outage tolerance.
- Scenarios (cyber, cloud outage, ransomware).
- Continuity plan: degraded modes, communications.
- BCP exercise: **yearly**.

## 2. Enhanced (regulated)
- Exercise: **semi-annually** for critical scope.

## 3. Evidence
- BIA, BCP plan, exercise reports.
