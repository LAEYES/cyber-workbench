# STD — D08 — Incident Disclosure Standard — license-safe

**Organization:** ACME  
**Version:** 0.2 (hardened)  
**Date:** 2026-02-03

## 1. Goal
Ensure incident notification obligations are met (authorities, customers, partners) with a clear process, timelines and evidence.

## 2. Baseline requirements
- Defined RACI and notification channels.
- Templates for authorities, customers, internal comms.
- Decision log: who decided, when, on what basis.
- Annual table-top exercise.

## 3. Enhanced requirements (regulated)
- Semi-annual exercises.
- Legal review integrated into IR runbooks.
- Timestamped timeline + retention of communication versions.

## 4. Audit criteria (pass/fail)
- [ ] Written procedure.
- [ ] RACI defined.
- [ ] Exercises performed and documented.
- [ ] Decision log retained.

## 5. Evidence
- Exercise report.
- Template samples.
- Decision log.
